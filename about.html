About us

The job site for refugees and companies looking to make a difference

Our mission

Our goal was to create a website that makes finding opportunities more accessible. Our website allows users to create a profile/portfolio of their interests and work preferences. Then from this data cactUs will recommend opportunites for the individuals. Furthermore, these can be used to help better match opportunities to each individual. This data will be securely saved and be kept up to date with UK data regulations. Additionally, cactUs will partner companies to help provide and make opportunites more accessible to people who are from less fortunate areas. This can be either to help refugees or immigrants find jobs; helping students find placement opportunites, and to create more comprehensive library of opportunites. Our website aims to make compnaies more aware of the difference that they can make in helping us cactii in bettering society.

Helping Refugees

One of our goals was to help refugees and immigrants adapt to the UK. This can include having a documentation on how to remain in the UK, finding free English courses, qualification converter, finding local communities and many more. This makes cactUs more accessible to people where English is not their first language, refugees and immigrants. Additionally we wanted to help improve Evironmental, Social and Governace in companies. As there there is still a lack of diversity in many fields. Whether this is a gender gap, lack in minority representation or even just being more aware of how the company can impact lives.
