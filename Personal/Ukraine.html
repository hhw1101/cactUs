Homes for Ukraine scheme

The Homes for Ukraine scheme is a sponsorship scheme where a ukraine national or their immediate family can come to the UK is they have named eligable UK sponsor.

To check the eligbility of your sponsor please visit the following site.

https://www.gov.uk/guidance/homes-for-ukraine-sponsor-guidance

To apply for this scheme you must fill out the form on the following page.

https://www.gov.uk/guidance/apply-for-a-visa-under-the-ukraine-sponsorship-scheme

Learning English

eTalk, an english language online school offers free english lessons for anyone over the age of 7 fleeing Ukraine. These courses are held over zoom twice a week and you can sign up here.
